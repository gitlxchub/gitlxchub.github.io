Research Interest

  • To study the prevalence, etiologies, and burden of DoC or other neurological diseases among children in home care using sample surveys or extrapolations from available registries.

  • Diagnosing and predicting the prognosis of DoC or neurodegenerative diseases using single or multi-modal techniques (EEG, fMRI, PET, etc.).

  • Using machine learning to study common neurophysiology and imaging features in different states of consciousness loss (concussion, sleep, anesthesia, seizure, etc.), and localizing key brain regions or dynamic networks for consciousness, neural correlates of consciousness.

  • Identifying common and differing neurological features in patients with Alzheimer's disease dementia and patients with DoC after brain injury.

  • Sleep and pain in patients with DoC.

  • Conducting clinical trials on therapies for DoC or other neurological diseases (e.g., hallucinogen, deep brain stimulation, hyperbaric oxygen therapy, etc.).

  • Exploring the mechanism of the binding problem.

  • Investigating the role of the cuneus and precuneus in the maintenance of consciousness.

  • Brain computer interface on DoC.

  • What is it like to be UWS or MCS? Do patients in UWS or MCS have a subjective feeling? Near death Experience.
